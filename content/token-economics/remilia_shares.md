Remilia Shares will be provided to Collective Artists and Founding Developers, and available for [sale to the public](token-economics/distribution) for investment.

Holding shares provides value through four mechanisms:
1. Shareholder Tiers: Benefits related to Remilia Projects are granted according to number of shares held in single wallet
2. Art Drops: Each Share acts as a lottery ticket for Remilia Art Drops
3. Dispatches: Each Shareholder wallet, containing at least one Remilia Share, will be eligible for Remilia Dispatches, such as Remilia Zine NFT, communications from Remilia Corp, or serial NFT art drops.
4. Emissions: Starting fund for $REMI Coin emissions
