Remilia Shares will be provided to Collective Artists and Founding Developers, and available for [sale to the public](token-economics/distribution) for investment.

Holding shares provides primary investment value through three mechanisms:
1. Art Drops: Each Share acts as a lottery ticket for Remilia Art Drops
2. Dispatches: Each Shareholder wallet, containing at least one Remilia Share, will be eligible for Remilia Dispatches, such as Remilia Zine NFT, communications from Remilia Corp, or serial NFT art drops.
3. Emissions: Starting fund for $REMI Coin emissions
4. Private Auctions: Limited-access first sale Remilia Market auctions, exclusive to high-tiered Shareholders (See below).
