Remilia Shares will be provided to Collective Artists and Founding Developers, and available for [sale to the public](token-economics/distribution) for investment.

Holding shares provides primary investment value through three mechanisms:
1. Art Drops: Each Share acts as a lottery ticket for Remilia Art Drops
2. Dispatches: Each Shareholder wallet, containing at least one Remilia Share, will be eligible for Remilia Dispatches, such as Remilia Zine NFT, communications from Remilia Corp, or serial NFT art drops.
3. Emissions: Starting fund for $REMI Coin emissions
4. Private Auctions: Limited-access first sale Remilia Market auctions, exclusive to high-tiered Shareholders (See below).

## Shareholder tiers:

Other benefits are allotted to Shareholders, distributed by Shareholder tiers. Shareholder tiers are determined by the number of Remilia Shares held in a single wallet.

Onyx - 1 Share: 
- **One (1) Ticket for Standard Art Drops**
- **One (1) Vote in the Patronage Fund**
- Access to Remilia Chat

Quartz - 5 Shares: 
- **Five (5) Tickets for Standard Art Drops**
- **Five (5) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **One (1) Ticket for Emerald NFT drops**
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- Access to Private Remilia Gallery

Emerald - 10 Shares:
- **Ten (10) Tickets for Standard Art Drops**
- **Ten (10) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **Two (2) Tickets for Emerald NFT drops**
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- *Access to Private Remilia Gallery*
- Access to Private Art Auctions
- Early access to Project Development

Jade - 20 Shares:
- **Twenty (20) Ticket sfor Standard Art Drops**
- **Five (5) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **Three (3) Ticket sfor Emerald NFT drops**
- **One (1) Ticket for Jadeite Grade Art drops
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- *Access to Private Remilia Gallery*
