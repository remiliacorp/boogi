Remilia Market is provided as a private digital art gallery for NFTs, exclusively curating Remilia Collective artist works. 

![](2021-03-21-22-50-29.png)

## Remilia Collective Exclusivity

Remilia Collective artists are contracted to present exclusively on our Collection, with first sales always taking place on Remilia Market.

The Market is tightly curated, with Remilia Corp controlling uploads and auctions in collaboration with the artists, with a focus on value preservation.

### Remilia Residency

Artists will be provided residency to show works in Remilia Market for the season. 

## Technical Specifications

Remilia Market uses the OpenSea's SDK framework to allow them to be lazy minted and compatible on the OpenSea's aggregation standard, with MetaMask integrated for ETH payments.

All Remilia Market art is backed up on OpenSea's, with NFTs able to be bought and sold from both Remilia Market's frontend and OpenSea.
