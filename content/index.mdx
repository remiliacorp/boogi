Remilia Documentation
=====================

Official project documentation of the Remilia Collective and related entities - WIP

## Corporate Structure

- Remilia Corp - Umbrella Company..
    - Remilia Collective - Artists committed to exclusive NFT representation by Remilia Collection, each holding 1 share in the Remilia Co-op.
        - Remilia Co-op - Co-op fund supported by a flat portion of Remilia Collection sales, matched by Remilia Corp. Interest on earnings redistributed quarterly to Remilia Collective members equally, up until Remilia Trust is formed.
        - Remilia Trust (Eventual) - Remilia Co-op will organize its funds into a perpetual trust paying out a perpetual livable salary from interest after necessary funding number is reached.
        - Remilia Colony (Eventual Eventual) - Following the formation of the Trust, Remilia Co-op grow its money in a full investment portfolio towards funding the colonization of a traditional Japanese village by Remilia Collective members.
        - Remilia Patron Fund (Eventual) - Patron fund contributed from Remilia Corp revenue for supporting artists and projects outside the Collective, distribution voted on quarterly by Remilia Co-op.
    - Remilia Market - NFT art marketplace exclusively representing Remilia Collective artists on commission.
        - Remilia Gallery (Eventual) - Virtual in-browser/VR gallery curating Remilia Collection shows.
        - Remilia Island
    - Remilia Projects
        - Remilia House - Print-on-demand artbook publishing house and webstore platform for Remilia Collective physical products.
    - Remilia Community - Selfhosted digital infrastructure for Remilia Collective, friends & family.
