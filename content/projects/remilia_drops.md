## Jade Cabbage

Take a cabbage from the store, scan it in 3D with an AR app, overlay a high resolution picture of Jade marble as texture, sell as one-of-a-kind digital Jade Cabbage -- destroy the real cabbage.

Ritual performed once a month and dropped in a lottery to one lucky RemCo Share. The store of Jade Cabbages that ends up back in RemCorp will be buyable for 3 Remilia Stars.

Given enough time, we will all own a Jade Cabbage.
